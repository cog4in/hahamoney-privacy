❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored? Is it secure?
All bookkeeping data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server. We do not collect your financial data, ensuring complete privacy and security.
Which currencies are supported?
We currently support major currencies including TWD (New Taiwan Dollar), HKD (Hong Kong Dollar), USD (US Dollar), CNY (Chinese Yuan), and JPY (Japanese Yen). You can switch the default currency in Settings.
Does the app require an internet connection?
HaHaMoney is a fully offline app. No account registration or internet connection is required to use all bookkeeping features. Network is only needed when checking for updates or sending feedback.
